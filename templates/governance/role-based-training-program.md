## 1. Executive Summary

*Provide a high-level overview of the training program, its strategic intent, and alignment to BNM RMiT requirements. Summarise key completion targets, program sponsors, and expected security culture outcomes. This section should be suitable for presentation to senior management and the Board.*

[Organization Name] is committed to maintaining a security-aware workforce that understands and actively supports its obligations under Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document. This Role-Based Training Program ("the Program") establishes a structured, risk-proportionate approach to technology risk and information security training, ensuring that all personnel — from Board members to operational staff — receive training commensurate with their responsibilities and access privileges.

The Program is designed to:

- Satisfy the mandatory training requirements under BNM RMiT Clause 15.3.
- Reduce the organisation's human-factor risk exposure through consistent and measurable training interventions.
- Foster a security-conscious culture across all levels of [Organization Name].
- Provide demonstrable evidence of compliance readiness for BNM supervisory reviews and internal audits.

This program is sponsored jointly by the **Head of Human Resources** and the **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)**, with oversight from the **Board Risk Committee** and **Senior Management**.

**Program Highlights:**

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Overall Staff Completion Rate | ≥ 95% annually |
| Pass Rate per Assessment | ≥ 80% per attempt |
| Maximum Remediation Attempts | 3 attempts per assessment cycle |
| Program Review Frequency | Annual (or upon material regulatory change) |
| Escalation Threshold (non-completion) | 30 days overdue |
