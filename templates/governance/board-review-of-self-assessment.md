## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document records the Board of Directors' formal review and sign-off of the annual Technology Risk Management self-assessment conducted by [Organization Name] in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 18.1**.

The self-assessment evaluates the organization's compliance posture, identifies gaps in technology risk controls, and provides the Board with an evidence-based basis for oversight decisions. This document serves as a primary governance artifact demonstrating Board-level accountability for technology risk management and is subject to regulatory inspection upon request.

**Scope of Review:**

- All technology systems, infrastructure, and processes within [Organization Name] as defined in the approved assessment scope
- Assessment period: [Assessment Period Start Date] to [Assessment Period End Date]
- Business units covered: [List of Business Units / Departments]
