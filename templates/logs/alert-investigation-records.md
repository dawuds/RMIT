## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document establishes the template and recording standards for **Alert Investigation Records** maintained by [Organization Name] in fulfilment of obligations under Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 10.39**.

Alert Investigation Records serve as the authoritative log of all Intrusion Detection System (IDS) and Intrusion Prevention System (IPS) alerts that have been received, triaged, and investigated by the IT Operations team. These records provide evidence of continuous monitoring activity, support incident response processes, and demonstrate regulatory compliance during examinations and audits.

### 1.2 Scope

This template applies to:

- All IDS/IPS alerts generated across [Organization Name]'s production, development, and disaster recovery environments
- Network-based intrusion detection (NIDS) and host-based intrusion detection (HIDS) alert sources
- All personnel involved in security monitoring, triage, escalation, and investigation activities
- Third-party managed security service providers (MSSPs) operating on behalf of [Organization Name]

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

| Regulatory Reference | Clause / Section | Requirement Summary |
|---|---|---|
| BNM RMiT Policy Document | 10.39 | Financial institutions shall implement and maintain effective monitoring of security events, including systematic logging and investigation of alerts generated by intrusion detection and prevention systems. |
