## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document constitutes the formal training materials and supporting evidence for the annual cybersecurity awareness programme conducted by [Organization Name]. It is maintained in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 15.1**, which mandates that financial institutions implement and maintain a structured cybersecurity awareness and training programme for all personnel.

These materials serve as the primary evidence artefact demonstrating compliance with the RMiT requirement that staff are equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to recognise, prevent, and respond to cybersecurity threats relevant to their roles. This document is subject to annual review and must be updated to reflect emerging threats, regulatory changes, and organisational developments.

**Scope of Application:**

- All permanent employees of [Organization Name]
- Contract staff with access to institutional systems or data
- Third-party vendors and service providers with privileged access (where applicable)
- Board members and Senior Management (targeted content, as applicable)

### 8.2 Trigger-Based Content Updates

In addition to the annual review, content shall be updated on a triggered basis under the following circumstances:

- Issuance of a new or revised BNM policy document, circular, or advisory
- Identification of a significant new threat vector (e.g., novel ransomware campaign targeting Malaysian FIs)
- Material internal security incident that reveals a training gap
- Findings from an internal or external audit identifying content deficiencies
- Request from the CISO or Board Risk Committee
