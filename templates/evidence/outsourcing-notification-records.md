## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official record of outsourcing notifications submitted to Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) by [Organization Name] in accordance with the **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically Clause 16.3. It provides a structured and auditable evidence trail demonstrating that [Organization Name] has fulfilled its regulatory obligation to notify BNM prior to, or upon, the engagement of material outsourcing arrangements involving technology services and systems.

This template governs the creation, maintenance, and retention of all outsourcing notification records as required by BNM. It ensures that notifications are complete, timely, and contain sufficient information to satisfy regulatory scrutiny.

### 1.2 Scope

*This section defines the boundaries of applicability. Identify the types of outsourcing arrangements, systems, and organizational units covered by this record.*

This record applies to:

- All **material outsourcing arrangements** as defined under BNM RMiT Clause 16.3, involving the outsourcing of technology functions, systems, or services to third-party vendors or service providers.
- Technology outsourcing engagements that affect the **confidentiality, integrity, or availability** of critical systems or customer data.
- Cloud services, managed security services, data processing, and technology infrastructure services procured from external parties.
- All business units and subsidiaries of [Organization Name] operating within Malaysia.

**Out of Scope:**

- Procurement of commoditized software licenses where no ongoing service management is involved.
- Internal shared services arrangements between wholly owned subsidiaries (unless classified as material outsourcing under BNM guidance).

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

| Regulation | Clause / Section | Obligation |
|---|---|---|
| BNM Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) | Clause 16.3 | Notification to BNM for material technology outsourcing arrangements |
| BNM Outsourcing Policy | [Applicable Section] | General outsourcing governance requirements |
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) | [Applicable Section] | Data protection obligations for outsourced data processing |

| RTO (Recovery Time Objective) | The maximum acceptable period of time following a disruption within which a business process or system must be restored. |
| RPO (Recovery Point Objective) | The maximum acceptable amount of data loss measured in time; the point in time to which data must be recoverable after an incident. |
| SLA (Service Level Agreement) | A contract between [Organization Name] and the vendor defining the expected level of service, performance metrics, and remedies for non-compliance. |
