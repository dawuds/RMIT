## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official register of board-level training sessions on technology risk and cybersecurity conducted at [Organization Name]. It provides an auditable record demonstrating that the Board of Directors maintains the requisite awareness and competency to discharge their governance obligations in relation to technology risk management.

This record supports compliance with **BNM Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 8.3**, which requires financial institutions to ensure that the board and senior management receive adequate and ongoing education on technology and cyber risks to enable effective oversight.

### 1.2 Scope

This record applies to:

- All members of the **Board of Directors** of [Organization Name]
- **Board Committee members** with technology or audit oversight responsibilities (e.g., Board Audit Committee, Board Risk Committee)
- **Senior Management** participating in board-directed technology risk training programmes

This record covers all formal training sessions, workshops, briefings, tabletop exercises, and awareness programmes conducted for the above audience, whether delivered internally or by external providers.

### 1.3 Regulatory Context

BNM RMiT Clause 8.3 places explicit responsibility on the board to maintain sufficient understanding of technology and cybersecurity risks to perform effective governance. This record provides the primary evidence artefact demonstrating fulfilment of that obligation during regulatory examinations and internal audits.
