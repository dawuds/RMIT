## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

*This section defines the intent of the document and its role within the organization's overall technology risk governance framework.*

This document establishes the **Staffing and Competency Records** for the Technology Risk Management function of [Organization Name] (hereinafter referred to as "the Institution"). It provides a structured record of current and planned staffing levels, required competency profiles, skills assessment outcomes, training plans, and progress against capability development milestones for all personnel with technology risk responsibilities.

This document is maintained in compliance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 8.7**, which requires financial institutions to ensure that staff responsible for technology risk management possess adequate qualifications, skills, and experience commensurate with the nature, scale, and complexity of the Institution's technology operations.

### 2.2 RMiT Clause 8.7 — Key Requirements

*Summarize the specific obligations under RMiT Clause 8.7 that this document is designed to address.*

RMiT Clause 8.7 requires the Institution to:

- Maintain a sufficient number of qualified staff to perform technology risk management functions effectively
- Ensure staff possess competencies commensurate with the nature, scale, and complexity of the Institution's technology environment
- Establish a structured programme to develop, maintain, and enhance technology risk competencies
- Document and periodically review staffing levels and competency requirements
- Ensure succession planning arrangements are in place for key technology risk roles
- Provide evidence of ongoing training, certification, and professional development for technology risk personnel
