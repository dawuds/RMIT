## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official Continuous Professional Development (CPD) Records template for IT audit personnel within [Organization Name]. It is maintained in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 13.3**, which mandates that financial institutions ensure their technology risk and audit functions are staffed by suitably qualified and continuously developed personnel.

This record provides an auditable trail of structured and unstructured learning activities undertaken by IT audit staff, demonstrating ongoing competence across technology risk, information security, application development oversight, and regulatory compliance domains.

### 1.2 Scope

This template applies to:

- All personnel performing IT audit, technology risk, or application security review functions
- Staff accountable under the IT audit universe, including those reviewing SDLC, code review, and security controls
- Third-party or seconded personnel performing equivalent IT audit duties on behalf of [Organization Name]

This record is system-generated and maintained continuously, with formal quarterly reviews conducted by the Head of Application Development.

> **Note:** Completion of CPD activities recorded herein is **not mandatory** under RMiT but is maintained as a best-practice evidence artifact demonstrating personnel competence and institutional commitment to ongoing capability development.

## 7. Finding Severity Classification Competency

*IT audit personnel must demonstrate competency in classifying audit findings by severity. This section records training and assessment activities that develop and validate this competency.*

### 7.1 Severity Classification Framework

*Document the finding severity classification scheme used by the IT audit function and the CPD activities that train personnel in its application.*

| Severity Level | Definition | Examples | CPD Training Requirement |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Immediate risk of significant financial loss, regulatory breach, or system compromise | Unpatched critical vulnerabilities, absence of MFA on privileged accounts | Annual refresher mandatory |
| **High** | Significant control weakness with material risk exposure | Inadequate access reviews, missing security testing gates | Annual refresher mandatory |
| **Medium** | Control gap with moderate risk; compensating controls may exist | Incomplete documentation, delayed patch application | Biennial refresher |
| **Low** | Minor deficiency with minimal risk impact | Process inefficiencies, documentation gaps | As part of general methodology training |
| **Informational** | Observation for management consideration; no immediate risk | Best practice recommendations | As part of general methodology training |
