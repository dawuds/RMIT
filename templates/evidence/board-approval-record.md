### 2.4 Objectives of the Technology Risk Management Framework

The TRMF is designed to achieve the following objectives:

1. **Regulatory Compliance:** Ensure full and demonstrable compliance with BNM RMiT, associated BNM guidelines, and all applicable Malaysian legislation governing technology risk in financial institutions.

2. **Risk Identification:** Establish a systematic, repeatable methodology for identifying technology risks across all asset categories and organisational functions, including emerging risks associated with new technologies and delivery models.

3. **Risk Assessment and Prioritisation:** Apply a consistent, quantitative and qualitative risk assessment methodology to evaluate the likelihood and impact of identified technology risks, enabling informed prioritisation of treatment activities.

4. **Risk Treatment:** Define and implement appropriate controls, mitigations, and risk transfer mechanisms commensurate with the organisation's risk appetite and the assessed severity of each identified risk.

5. **Governance and Accountability:** Establish clear lines of ownership, accountability, and escalation for technology risk across the three lines of defence model.

6. **Resilience and Continuity:** Ensure that critical technology systems are designed and operated to meet defined availability, recovery time, and recovery point objectives, and that business continuity and disaster recovery plans are maintained and tested.

7. **Third-Party Risk Management:** Extend technology risk governance to material third-party service providers through due diligence, contractual protections, and ongoing monitoring.

8. **Continuous Improvement:** Embed a culture of continuous improvement in technology risk management, informed by internal audit findings, industry developments, regulatory guidance, and lessons learned from technology incidents.

#### Phase 3: Risk Treatment

*Document the risk treatment options available and the criteria for selecting each.*

For each identified and assessed technology risk, the designated risk owner must select one or more of the following treatment options:

| Treatment Option | Description | Conditions for Use | Approval Required |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mitigate (Reduce)** | Implement controls to reduce likelihood and/or impact to acceptable levels | Risk can be brought within appetite through feasible controls | Risk Owner + CRO |
| **Transfer** | Transfer risk to a third party (e.g., insurance, contractual provisions) | Residual risk cannot be fully mitigated; third party is better positioned to manage | CRO + CFO |
| **Avoid** | Cease the activity that gives rise to the risk | Risk exceeds appetite and cannot be reduced or transferred at reasonable cost | CRO + CEO / Board |
| **Accept** | Accept the residual risk without further treatment | Residual risk is within appetite; cost of further mitigation exceeds benefit | CRO (Low/Medium); Board (High/Critical) |

All treatment decisions must be documented in the Technology Risk Register ([Document reference]) and reviewed at the frequency appropriate to the risk rating.
