## 1. Purpose and Scope

*This section describes the intent of this document and the organisational boundaries it covers.*

This document presents the formal organisational structure of **[Organisation Name]** as it pertains to technology risk governance and oversight. It serves as evidence that the risk management function operates with appropriate independence from IT operations, in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document, Clause 8.7**, which requires financial institutions to establish clear lines of accountability and segregation of duties between technology risk oversight and operational technology functions.

**Scope:** This chart covers all divisions, departments, and reporting lines relevant to:

- Technology risk management and governance
- IT operations and service delivery
- Internal audit and compliance oversight
- Senior management and Board-level technology risk committees

## 2. Assessment Scope and Criteria

*Define the perimeter of the organisational assessment and the criteria used to evaluate independence and adequacy of the risk function structure.*

### 2.1 Scope Boundary

The organisational chart assessment encompasses the following units:

| Unit / Division | Included in Scope | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Board Risk Committee (BRC) | Yes | Highest oversight authority for technology risk |
| Board Audit Committee (BAC) | Yes | Independent audit oversight |
| Chief Risk Officer (CRO) / Risk Division | Yes | Primary subject of independence assessment |
| Chief Information Officer (CIO) / IT Division | Yes | Counterpart to risk function |
| Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) | Yes | Cybersecurity risk governance |
| Internal Audit (IT Audit) | Yes | Second/third line assurance |
| Technology Operations | Yes | Day-to-day IT delivery |
| [Other Department] | [Yes / No] | [Rationale] |

### 2.2 Assessment Criteria

Independence of the risk function is assessed against the following criteria:

- **Reporting line separation:** The Head of Technology Risk does not report to the CIO or any IT operations head.
- **Budgetary independence:** The technology risk function controls its own budget allocation without IT operations influence.
- **Mandate clarity:** Roles and responsibilities are formally documented with no dual-hatting between risk oversight and operations.
- **Escalation path:** The risk function has a direct escalation path to the Board or Board Risk Committee, bypassing IT management.
- **Regulatory alignment:** Structure meets BNM RMiT Clause 8.7 requirements for independence and accountability.

## 3. Methodology Used

*Describe the approach taken to develop, validate, and maintain this organisational chart and independence assessment.*

The organisational structure documented herein was derived using the following methodology:

1. **Data Collection:** HR records, employment contracts, job descriptions, and committee Terms of Reference (ToR) were reviewed to confirm actual reporting lines.
2. **Interviews:** Structured interviews were conducted with [Head of HR], [CRO], and [CIO] to validate functional versus administrative reporting distinctions.
3. **Gap Analysis:** Current structure was benchmarked against BNM RMiT Clause 8.7 requirements and industry best practice (e.g., COBIT 2019, ISO 31000).
4. **Validation:** Draft charts were reviewed by Internal Audit and Legal/Compliance for accuracy and regulatory adequacy.
5. **Approval:** Final structure was approved by [Approved By] on [Approval Date].
