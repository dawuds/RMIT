## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document serves as the official record of the annual vendor risk reassessment conducted by [Organization Name] in accordance with Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 10.46**, which mandates that financial institutions periodically reassess the risk profile of technology service providers and critical vendors.

The scope of this document covers all third-party vendors classified as **critical** or **significant** to the technology operations, data processing, and information security posture of [Organization Name]. Reassessments are conducted on an annual basis, or more frequently where material changes in vendor risk profile are identified.

This record provides auditable evidence that [Organization Name] has fulfilled its obligations with respect to ongoing vendor due diligence, risk tiering, and exit strategy preparedness as required under BNM RMiT.
