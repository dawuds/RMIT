## 1. Purpose and Scope

*This section defines the intent and boundaries of this document. Describe the regulatory obligation being fulfilled and the organizational scope to which this record applies.*

This document records the planning, execution, observations, and outcomes of cyber crisis simulations conducted by [Organization Name] in fulfilment of **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 11.5**, which mandates that financial institutions conduct periodic crisis simulation exercises to assess the effectiveness of their cyber incident response and business continuity capabilities.

**Scope:** This record covers all cyber crisis simulation exercises conducted within the annual review cycle ending **[Review Cycle End Date]**. It applies to:

- All critical technology systems and supporting infrastructure within [Organization Name]
- Business functions and departments designated as critical under the institution's Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
- Third-party service providers and outsourced functions participating in simulation exercises where applicable

**Out of Scope:** [Define any systems, departments, or scenarios explicitly excluded from this record]
