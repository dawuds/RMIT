## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the formal record of risk owner acknowledgements for technology-related risks identified and managed by [Organization Name] in accordance with Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT)** Policy Document. Specifically, this artifact provides auditable evidence that risk owners have reviewed, acknowledged, and accepted accountability for risks assigned to them, as required under **RMiT Clause 9.5**.

These records demonstrate that [Organization Name] maintains a governed and accountable technology risk management framework, with clearly assigned ownership and documented sign-off at the appropriate organisational level.

### 1.2 Scope

*Define the organisational and system boundaries covered by these sign-off records. Include all relevant departments, technology assets, and risk registers in scope.*

This template applies to:

- All technology risks identified within [Organization Name]'s Technology Risk Register
- All systems, platforms, and infrastructure assets within the scope of the RMiT policy
- Risk owners at the department head level and above
- Risks classified under the following domains: [e.g., Cybersecurity, IT Operations, Third-Party Technology, Data Management, Business Continuity]

**Out of Scope:**

- Operational risks not pertaining to technology
- Risks managed under separate frameworks (e.g., credit risk, market risk) unless they intersect with technology risk

### 1.3 Regulatory Context

| Regulation / Standard | Clause / Section | Requirement Summary |
|---|---|---|
| BNM RMiT Policy Document | Clause 9.5 | Risk owners must formally acknowledge assigned technology risks, including acceptance of accountability for risk treatment and residual risk |
| BNM RMiT Policy Document | Clause 9 (General) | Establishment of a technology risk management structure with defined ownership |
| [Other applicable BNM Policy / Act] | [Clause] | [Brief requirement summary] |

## 4. Owner and Custodian

*This section formally documents the accountability structure for each risk item. Risk ownership must be assigned to a named individual at the appropriate seniority level consistent with BNM RMiT Clause 9.5 requirements.*

### 4.1 Ownership Definitions

| Role | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Risk Owner** | The individual accountable for managing the risk to within appetite. Must be at Department Head level or above. Responsible for approving the risk treatment plan and formally acknowledging residual risk. |
| **Risk Custodian** | The individual responsible for day-to-day monitoring and management of controls associated with the risk. Typically a senior manager or system administrator. |
| **Risk Coordinator** | The individual responsible for maintaining the accuracy of the risk register entry and facilitating the sign-off process. Typically from the Risk or Compliance function. |
| **Technology Asset Owner** | The individual accountable for the security, availability, and lifecycle management of the associated technology asset. |

## 6. Last Review Date and Sign-off Log

*This is the primary evidence section for BNM RMiT Clause 9.5 compliance. Each risk owner must complete a formal sign-off entry at least quarterly, or upon any material change event. This log is the authoritative audit trail for risk owner acknowledgements.*

### 6.1 Trigger Events for Sign-off

*Sign-off is required upon any of the following trigger events:*

- Scheduled quarterly review cycle (at minimum)
- New risk identified and entered into the Technology Risk Register
- Material change to a risk's likelihood, impact, or residual rating
- Completion or failure of a risk treatment action
- Change in risk ownership (new owner must sign off upon assignment)
- Following a technology incident or near-miss with risk register implications
- Prior to the submission of the annual Technology Risk Report to senior management or the Board
- Regulatory examination, internal audit, or external assessment request
