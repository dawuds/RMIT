## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the formal record of risk acceptance decisions made by [Organization Name] in relation to identified technology risks. Each entry represents a deliberate, documented decision by an authorized party to accept a residual risk that cannot be fully mitigated within operational or budgetary constraints. These records provide auditable evidence of risk governance and accountability in accordance with Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) policy document.

### 1.2 Scope

This template applies to all technology-related risks identified within [Organization Name] that have been assessed, evaluated, and formally accepted rather than mitigated, transferred, or avoided. It covers risks associated with information systems, technology infrastructure, digital services, third-party technology dependencies, and operational technology within the scope of BNM RMiT.

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

This artifact fulfills the requirements of:

- **BNM RMiT, Paragraph 9.3** — Risk acceptance decisions must be formally documented, approved by an appropriate authority commensurate with the risk level, and subject to periodic review to ensure continued validity of the acceptance rationale.

Risk acceptance records must be maintained for audit purposes and made available to BNM upon request.

## 3. Roles and Responsibilities

*This section defines the roles involved in the creation, maintenance, approval, and audit of Risk Acceptance Records. Accountability is assigned in accordance with [Organization Name]'s governance framework and BNM RMiT requirements.*

| Role | Responsible (R) | Accountable (A) | Consulted (C) | Informed (I) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Risk Owner (Department Head)** | Initiates and maintains the record; monitors the accepted risk | Accountable for ongoing risk exposure and timely review | Consulted on compensating controls and escalation triggers | Informed of changes to risk status |
| **Risk Custodian** | Day-to-day monitoring of KRIs and compensating controls | — | Consulted on operational feasibility of controls | Informed of acceptance decisions |
| **Chief Risk Officer (CRO) / Risk Management Function** | Reviews and validates risk rating and classification | Accountable for the integrity of the risk acceptance process | Consulted on methodology and risk appetite alignment | Informed of all High and Critical acceptances |
| **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)** | Validates cybersecurity risk classifications and compensating controls | Accountable for technology risk acceptance decisions within cybersecurity domain | Consulted on threat landscape and control adequacy | Informed of all technology risk acceptances |
| **Internal Audit** | Conducts independent review of risk acceptance records during audits | — | — | Informed of all active risk acceptances |
| **Compliance Function** | Reviews regulatory implications of accepted risks | — | Consulted on BNM RMiT and PDPA compliance obligations | Informed of risks with regulatory exposure |
| **Board Risk Committee** | Reviews and endorses risk acceptances escalated per policy | Accountable for oversight of enterprise risk profile | Consulted on strategic risk appetite | Informed of all High and Critical acceptances |
| **Record Management / GRC System Administrator** | Maintains records in the GRC system; ensures completeness and retention | Accountable for record integrity and system accuracy | — | Informed of new and updated records |

### Appendix C: Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Risk Acceptance** | A risk treatment option where [Organization Name] decides to retain the risk without further mitigation, based on an informed decision that the residual risk is within the defined risk appetite. |
| **Residual Risk** | The risk remaining after risk treatment controls have been applied. |
| **Risk Owner** | The individual accountable for ensuring that the risk is managed appropriately and that the acceptance record is kept current. |
| **Risk Custodian** | The individual responsible for the day-to-day operational management of the risk and its associated controls. |
| **Compensating Control** | A safeguard or countermeasure employed in the absence of a recommended control, to provide equivalent or comparable protection. |
| **KRI** | Key Risk Indicator — a metric used to signal increasing risk exposure before a risk event occurs. |
| **RMiT** | Risk Management in Technology — BNM's policy document governing technology risk management for financial institutions. |
