## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document serves as the formal template and record for Vendor Performance Reviews conducted by [Organization Name] in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 10.48**, which mandates that financial institutions maintain structured oversight of technology service providers, including regular assessment of service levels, risk posture, and contractual compliance.

**Purpose:** To provide a standardized, audit-ready record of vendor performance evaluations that supports informed decision-making, regulatory compliance, and continuous service improvement.

**Scope:** This template applies to all third-party technology vendors and service providers engaged by [Organization Name], including but not limited to:

- Cloud service providers (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS)
- Managed security service providers (MSSPs)
- Technology infrastructure and maintenance vendors
- Software development and support vendors
- Data processing and storage service providers

**Review Triggers:** Reviews shall be conducted:
- **Quarterly** — as part of the regular vendor management cycle
- **Per-event** — triggered by SLA breaches, security incidents, material service disruptions, or contractual changes
