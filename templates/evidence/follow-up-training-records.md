## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official record of follow-up training activities conducted for personnel who failed phishing simulation exercises within [Organisation Name]. It provides auditable evidence that the organisation has implemented corrective measures in response to identified awareness gaps, in fulfilment of obligations under **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 15.2**, which mandates the establishment of ongoing and targeted cybersecurity awareness programmes proportionate to identified risks.

These records demonstrate that [Organisation Name] maintains an active, risk-responsive training posture and that deficiencies identified through phishing simulations are remediated in a timely, documented, and measurable manner.

### 1.2 Scope

This record applies to:

- All employees of [Organisation Name], including permanent staff, contract personnel, and third-party users with access to institutional systems, who have failed one or more phishing simulation exercises.
- All business units, subsidiaries, and branch offices within the regulatory perimeter of [Organisation Name].
- Follow-up training events triggered on a **per-event basis** (immediately following a failed simulation) and reviewed on a **quarterly basis** for trend analysis and programme effectiveness.
