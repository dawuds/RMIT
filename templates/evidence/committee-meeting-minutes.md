## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document records the proceedings of the Technology Risk Committee (TRC) meeting held by [Organization Name] in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 8.2**, which mandates the establishment of governance structures for the oversight of technology risk management.

These minutes serve as formal evidence of the Committee's deliberations, decisions, and actions taken to ensure the effective management of technology risks across the organization. They are subject to periodic review and must be retained as part of [Organization Name]'s regulatory evidence repository.

**Scope:** All technology risk matters, including but not limited to cybersecurity, IT operations, digital infrastructure, third-party technology risk, and technology-related incidents, within [Organization Name].
