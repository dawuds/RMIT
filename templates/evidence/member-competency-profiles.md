## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document establishes the Member Competency Profiles for [Organization Name]'s technology-related governance committees. It serves as an evidence artifact under Bank Negara Malaysia's **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT)** Policy Document, specifically addressing the requirements under **Clause 8.2**, which mandates that committee members collectively possess adequate technology competency to discharge their oversight responsibilities effectively.

**Scope:** This register covers all individuals appointed to technology governance bodies within [Organization Name], including but not limited to the Board Risk Committee, IT Steering Committee, Cybersecurity Committee, and any other committee with material technology oversight responsibilities. It captures competency evidence in the form of curriculum vitae, professional certifications, training records, and qualifications demonstrating relevant technology expertise.

> This document is designated as a **system-generated, continuously maintained evidence record**. It is subject to quarterly review and must be updated upon any change in committee membership, certification status, or role assignment.
