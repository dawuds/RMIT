## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document serves as the official record of escalations for overdue or unresolved technology risk findings to senior management and the Audit Committee of [Organization Name]. It is maintained in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 13.4**, which requires financial institutions to establish a structured escalation mechanism ensuring that material technology risk findings receive timely senior-level attention and remediation oversight.

**Scope:** This record applies to all technology risk and audit findings — including internal audit, external audit, regulatory examination, and continuous monitoring outputs — that have breached their agreed remediation deadlines or have been assessed as requiring immediate escalation due to severity. It covers all systems, infrastructure, and processes within the technology risk boundary of [Organization Name].

> **Note:** This is a system-generated, per-event record. A consolidated view is produced quarterly for Audit Committee reporting.
