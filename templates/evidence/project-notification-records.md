## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the authoritative register and evidence record for all technology project notifications submitted to Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM) by [Organization Name] in accordance with the BNM Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document, specifically Paragraph 16.2. It provides a structured, auditable record of notification submissions, ensuring full regulatory traceability and compliance.

This register supports [Organization Name]'s obligations to notify BNM of technology initiatives that meet prescribed criteria, and maintains evidence of timely submission through the BNM LINK regulatory reporting portal.

### 1.2 Scope

This register applies to:

- All technology projects initiated, in-flight, or completed within [Organization Name] that meet BNM LINK notification thresholds.
- All material technology initiatives, including but not limited to core banking system changes, cloud adoption, outsourcing arrangements, and critical infrastructure upgrades.
- All business units, technology departments, and third-party service providers operating under [Organization Name]'s governance framework.

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

This record is maintained pursuant to:

- **BNM RMiT**, Paragraph **16.2** — requirements for financial institutions to notify BNM of technology projects meeting defined materiality criteria.
- BNM LINK Submission Guidelines (current version as published by BNM).

> **Note:** This register is classified as **mandatory evidence** under the RMiT compliance programme. Non-maintenance or gaps in this register may constitute a reportable compliance breach.
