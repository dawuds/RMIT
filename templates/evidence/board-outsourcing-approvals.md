## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document records the Board of Directors' formal approval of material outsourcing arrangements entered into by [Organization Name], in accordance with Bank Negara Malaysia's **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 10.47**, as well as applicable provisions under the **Financial Services Act 2013 (FSA)**, **Islamic Financial Services Act 2013 (IFSA)**, and the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**.

**Purpose:** To provide a centralized, auditable record of all Board-level decisions regarding material outsourcing, ensuring that governance, accountability, and regulatory obligations are fulfilled prior to and throughout the lifecycle of each outsourcing arrangement.

**Scope:** This template applies to all material outsourcing arrangements as defined under RMiT, including but not limited to:

- Technology service providers hosting or processing critical systems or data
- Cloud service providers managing regulated data or core banking functions
- Third-party vendors performing functions that, if disrupted, would materially impact [Organization Name]'s operations, reputation, or compliance obligations

**Out of Scope:** Non-material vendor engagements, standard procurement activities, and internal shared-service arrangements that do not meet the materiality threshold defined in Section 3.3.
