## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official record template for developer training activities relating to secure coding practices conducted by [Organization Name]. It provides a structured and auditable evidence trail demonstrating compliance with Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically under **Clause 10.6**, which mandates that financial institutions ensure technology personnel maintain adequate competency in secure application development.

These records are a mandatory compliance artefact and must be maintained, reviewed, and made available upon regulatory request or internal audit.

### 1.2 Scope

This template applies to:

- All internal software developers, application engineers, and technical leads employed by [Organization Name]
- Contract developers, third-party developers, and outsourced development personnel with access to [Organization Name] systems or source code repositories
- Training programmes covering secure coding practices, application security, vulnerability management, and related technical disciplines
- All development projects classified under the organisation's application portfolio

### 1.3 Out of Scope

- General IT awareness training not specific to secure development practices (refer to [IT Security Awareness Training Records Document ID])
- Infrastructure or operations team training (refer to [Infrastructure Training Records Document ID])
