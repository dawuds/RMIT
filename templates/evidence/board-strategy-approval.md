## 1. Purpose and Scope

It evidences that the Board has reviewed, deliberated upon, and endorsed the cybersecurity strategic direction of the organization in accordance with the requirements set out under **Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 11.2**.

- Demonstrating active Board-level ownership and accountability for cybersecurity strategy

### 6.2 Risk-Based Approach

*Describe how the cybersecurity strategy is informed by risk — including the risk assessment methodology, threat modelling approach, and how risk informs control prioritisation.*

The cybersecurity programme adopts a **risk-based approach** in accordance with BNM RMiT requirements. The following methodology governs how cyber risks are identified, assessed, treated, and monitored:

#### 6.2.1 Threat Intelligence-Led Risk Assessment

- **Threat Identification:** [Organization Name] maintains an active threat intelligence function that monitors the Malaysian and global financial sector threat landscape, incorporating feeds from [FinanceISAC / NACSA / BNM Threat Intel / Commercial CTIP providers]
- **Threat Modelling:** The MITRE ATT&CK framework is used to model adversary tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) relevant to [Organization Name]'s risk profile
- **Risk Assessment Methodology:** Cyber risks are assessed using a [Qualitative / Quantitative / Hybrid] methodology, aligned with the organization's enterprise risk management (ERM) framework, using a **[5x5 / 3x3]** risk matrix

#### 6.2.2 Risk Assessment Matrix

| Likelihood | Negligible Impact | Minor Impact | Moderate Impact | Significant Impact | Critical Impact |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Almost Certain** | Medium | High | High | Critical | Critical |
| **Likely** | Low | Medium | High | High | Critical |
| **Possible** | Low | Medium | Medium | High | High |
| **Unlikely** | Low | Low | Medium | Medium | High |
| **Rare** | Low | Low | Low | Medium | Medium |

#### 6.2.3 Risk Treatment Options

| Risk Treatment | Description | Approval Required |
|---|---|---|
| **Mitigate** | Implement controls to reduce likelihood or impact | Risk Owner |
| **Accept** | Accept residual risk within appetite | [CRO / Risk Committee / Board] depending on risk level |
| **Transfer** | Transfer risk via insurance or contractual mechanisms | CRO |
| **Avoid** | Cease the activity generating the risk | EXCO / Board |

**Responsibilities:**
- Champion cybersecurity as a strategic and organizational priority
- Ensure CISO has appropriate authority, resources, and access to the Board

- Provide second-line oversight of technology and cybersecurity risk
