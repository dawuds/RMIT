## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document records the formal minutes of the Board of Directors of **[Organization Name]** for meetings at which technology risk matters are discussed, deliberated, and resolved. These minutes serve as primary evidence of Board-level governance over technology risk in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 8.1**, which requires the Board to provide oversight and strategic direction on technology risk management.

These minutes are a mandatory compliance artifact and must be retained as evidence for BNM supervisory reviews, internal audits, and regulatory examinations. All Board meetings at which technology risk agenda items appear are in scope.
