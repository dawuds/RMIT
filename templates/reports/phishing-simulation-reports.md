## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

*This section defines the purpose of this report and the regulatory mandate under which it is produced. Authors should clearly articulate the objective of the phishing simulation programme and how this report supports the organisation's security posture and regulatory obligations.*

This Phishing Simulation Report documents the results, findings, and recommendations arising from the quarterly phishing simulation exercise conducted by [Organization Name] for the reporting period [Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4] [Year]. The report is produced in accordance with the requirements of Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy**, specifically **Clause 15.2**, which mandates that financial institutions implement and evaluate security awareness programmes, including simulated phishing campaigns, to assess employee susceptibility to social engineering attacks.

The report provides:

- A quantitative and qualitative assessment of employee phishing susceptibility across [Organization Name]
- Identification of high-risk departments, user groups, and behavioural patterns
- Evidence of continuous security awareness programme effectiveness
- Actionable recommendations to reduce human-factor cyber risk
- A record of management response and committed remediation actions

### 3.4 Measurement Methodology

*Define exactly how each metric was calculated. This ensures reproducibility and audit integrity.*

The following metrics were captured and calculated for each simulation scenario:

| Metric | Definition | Calculation Method |
|---|---|---|
| **Click Rate** | Percentage of targeted users who clicked a simulated phishing link | (Users who clicked ÷ Total targeted users) × 100 |
| **Data Submission Rate** | Percentage of targeted users who submitted data (e.g., credentials) on a simulated landing page | (Users who submitted data ÷ Total targeted users) × 100 |
| **Reporting Rate** | Percentage of targeted users who reported the simulation email via the designated reporting mechanism | (Users who reported ÷ Total targeted users) × 100 |
| **Mean Time to Click (MTTC)** | Average time elapsed between email delivery and first click | Aggregated from platform tracking logs |
| **Mean Time to Report (MTTR)** | Average time elapsed between email delivery and user report | Aggregated from platform tracking logs |
| **Repeat Clicker Rate** | Percentage of users who clicked phishing simulations in two or more consecutive quarters | Cross-referenced against prior quarter records |
