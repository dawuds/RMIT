## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

This Training Completion Report is a mandatory compliance artifact issued by [Organization Name] in fulfilment of obligations under **Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 15.1**, which requires financial institutions to implement and maintain a structured technology risk awareness and competency programme for all personnel.

This report serves the following objectives:

- To provide evidence of [Organization Name]'s compliance with BNM RMiT Clause 15.1 personnel training requirements.
- To document the completion rates of mandatory and role-based technology risk training programmes across all staff categories.
- To identify non-compliance gaps and track remediation actions to closure.
- To inform senior management and the Board Risk Committee of the organisation's training posture and emerging competency risks.
- To support internal and external audit reviews, regulatory examinations, and independent assessments.

### 7.4 Training Effectiveness Review

In addition to completion rates and assessment scores, training effectiveness is evaluated through the following mechanisms:

- **Post-Training Surveys:** Participant satisfaction and self-assessed comprehension surveys administered after each module. Results are reviewed by the Training Team and reported to the Head of HR.
- **Phishing Simulation Results:** Click-through and reporting rates from quarterly phishing simulations serve as a behavioural indicator of cybersecurity awareness effectiveness.
- **Incident Root Cause Analysis:** Where security incidents are attributed to human error or lack of awareness, findings are fed back into training content review.
- **Annual Training Effectiveness Review:** The CISO and Head of HR conduct a joint annual review of the overall training programme's effectiveness, benchmarked against industry standards and regulatory expectations.
