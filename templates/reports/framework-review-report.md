## 5. Methodology and Approach

*Describe the systematic approach used to conduct the annual review, including the review methodology, evidence gathering techniques, assessment criteria, and rating scales used. This section is important for providing transparency and reproducibility of the review process.*

### 5.1 Review Methodology Overview

The Annual RMiT Framework Review was conducted using a structured, risk-based methodology comprising the following phases:

| Phase | Description | Duration | Key Activities |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Phase 1: Planning** | Define scope, objectives, and review approach | [e.g., 2 weeks] | Scope definition; stakeholder identification; document request; review team formation |
| **Phase 2: Document Review** | Review of policies, procedures, and supporting documentation | [e.g., 4 weeks] | Policy document review; gap analysis against RMiT requirements; prior year findings review |
| **Phase 3: Interviews and Walkthroughs** | Stakeholder interviews and process walkthroughs | [e.g., 3 weeks] | One-on-one interviews; process walkthroughs; control demonstrations |
| **Phase 4: Testing and Validation** | Testing of key controls and validation of evidence | [e.g., 4 weeks] | Control testing; sample testing; evidence review |
| **Phase 5: Analysis and Reporting** | Analysis of findings and preparation of this report | [e.g., 3 weeks] | Gap analysis; risk rating; recommendation development; report preparation |
| **Phase 6: Management Review** | Review of draft report by management | [e.g., 2 weeks] | Management review; factual accuracy check; management response collection |
| **Phase 7: Finalisation and Submission** | Finalisation of report and submission for approval | [e.g., 1 week] | Final report preparation; BRC presentation; formal approval |

### 5.2 Evidence Gathering Techniques

*Describe the types of evidence gathered during the review.*

The review team employed the following evidence-gathering techniques:

1. **Document Review**
   - Review of Board and committee minutes and papers
   - Review of existing policies, standards, procedures, and guidelines
   - Review of technology risk reports, dashboards, and MI packs
   - Review of incident logs and post-incident review reports
   - Review of audit reports (internal and external) and management letters
   - Review of vendor contracts and service level agreements (SLAs)
   - Review of training records and awareness materials

2. **Stakeholder Interviews**
   - Structured interviews with key stakeholders across the first, second, and third lines of defence
   - Focus groups with operational teams responsible for implementing framework requirements

3. **Process Walkthroughs**
   - Walkthroughs of key technology risk management processes
   - Demonstrations of risk management tools and systems

4. **Control Testing**
   - Sample-based testing of key preventive and detective controls
   - Review of automated control outputs and exception reports
   - Verification of control design against documented procedures

5. **Benchmarking**
   - Comparison of [Organization Name]'s framework against peer institutions and industry best practices
   - Review of BNM supervisory feedback and industry-wide thematic reviews
