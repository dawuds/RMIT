## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document establishes the **KRI Monitoring Dashboard** for [Organization Name], providing a structured and continuous view of Key Risk Indicators (KRIs) pertaining to technology risk management. The dashboard serves as a primary governance instrument for the Board of Directors and Senior Management to monitor, assess, and respond to technology-related risks in a timely and informed manner.

This artifact is produced in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 9.4**, which mandates financial institutions to establish metrics and indicators that enable ongoing monitoring of technology risks and the effectiveness of controls.

### 1.2 Scope

*Define the organisational and system boundaries that this KRI Monitoring Dashboard covers. Specify the business units, technology domains, and geographic coverage included.*

This dashboard covers the following domains within [Organization Name]:

- **Technology Infrastructure** — Networks, servers, data centres, and cloud environments
- **Information Security** — Cybersecurity controls, threat detection, and incident management
- **IT Operations** — System availability, change management, and capacity management
- **Third-Party and Outsourcing Risk** — Vendor and cloud service provider performance
- **Data Management** — Data integrity, backup, recovery, and data governance
- **Application Risk** — Core banking systems, digital channels, and business applications
- **Cyber Resilience** — Business continuity, disaster recovery, and cyber incident response

*Briefly describe the regulatory obligations driving this dashboard. Reference all applicable BNM policy documents and clauses.*

## 3. Scope and Methodology

### 3.1 KRI Selection Methodology

*Describe the process by which KRIs were selected, validated, and approved for inclusion in this dashboard. Explain the link between KRIs, the risk register, and the organisation's risk appetite statement.*

KRIs included in this dashboard were selected through the following process:

1. **Risk Appetite Alignment** — KRIs are mapped directly to [Organization Name]'s Board-approved Technology Risk Appetite Statement, ensuring that each indicator measures a risk dimension that the Board has explicitly defined tolerance levels for.
2. **Regulatory Mapping** — KRIs are aligned to RMiT requirements, particularly Clause 9.4, and reviewed against BNM thematic findings and supervisory expectations.
3. **Materiality Assessment** — KRIs are prioritised based on the potential financial, operational, reputational, and regulatory impact of a threshold breach.
4. **Stakeholder Validation** — KRI definitions, thresholds, and data sources are validated by the Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), Chief Risk Officer (CRO), and Head of IT Operations prior to Board approval.
5. **Annual Review** — The full KRI inventory is subject to annual review and re-approval by the Board Risk Committee.

### 3.2 Threshold Setting

*Explain how Green, Amber, and Red thresholds are established and who has authority to adjust them.*

Thresholds for each KRI are defined as follows:

| Status | Colour | Definition |
|---|---|---|
| **Within Appetite** | Green | The measured value is within the Board-approved risk appetite boundary. No escalation required. Continuous monitoring maintained. |
| **Approaching Threshold** | Amber | The measured value is approaching the risk appetite boundary. Management attention and pre-emptive action required. Report to Technology Risk Committee. |
| **Threshold Breached** | Red | The measured value has exceeded the risk appetite boundary. Immediate escalation to Board or Board Risk Committee required. Remediation plan to be submitted within [X] business days. |

Threshold values are:
- Proposed by the **Chief Risk Officer** in consultation with the **CISO** and **Head of IT Operations**
- Reviewed for benchmarking against industry peers and BNM supervisory guidance
- Approved by the **Board Risk Committee** on an annual basis or upon material change
- Documented in the **KRI Definition Register** (see Appendix A)
