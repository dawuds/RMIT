## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

This **Quarterly Board Risk Report — Technology Risk Management** is a mandatory governance document prepared in accordance with Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) **Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 8.5**, which requires financial institutions to establish mechanisms for reporting technology risk information to the Board of Directors at appropriate intervals.

This report is designed to:

- Provide the Board with a comprehensive, accurate, and timely view of the organisation's technology risk landscape for the quarter ended [Reporting Period End Date].
- Enable the Board to fulfil its oversight responsibilities with respect to technology risk governance as prescribed by RMiT.
- Facilitate informed Board-level decision-making on technology risk appetite, resource allocation, and strategic risk mitigation priorities.
- Demonstrate the organisation's accountability and transparency to BNM and other relevant regulatory bodies including the **National Cyber Security Agency (NACSA)** and obligations under the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**.
- Document a formal record of Board awareness and deliberation on technology risks for supervisory and audit purposes.

### 2.2 Scope

This report covers technology risk across the following dimensions:

- **Organisational Scope:** All business units, subsidiaries, branches, and representative offices of [Organization Name] operating under the purview of BNM licensing.
- **Technology Scope:** All information technology systems, digital infrastructure, data assets, technology-enabled products and services, and third-party technology service providers supporting the operations of [Organization Name].
- **Risk Domains Covered:**
  - Cyber and information security risk
  - Technology resilience and availability risk
  - Data governance and protection risk
  - Technology project and change risk
  - Third-party technology risk
  - Emerging technology risk
  - Technology asset lifecycle risk

### 2.3 Regulatory Basis

This report is prepared to fulfil the requirements of the following regulatory instruments:

| Regulation | Issuing Authority | Relevant Clause(s) |
|---|---|---|
| Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) | Bank Negara Malaysia | Clause 8.5 |
| Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) | Bank Negara Malaysia | Clauses 10.1–10.4 (Cyber Risk) |
| Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) | Bank Negara Malaysia | Clause 11.1 (Technology Resilience) |
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) | Ministry of Digital | Sections 9–12 |
| NACSA Cyber Security Framework | NACSA | [Applicable Sections] |
| [Other internal policy, e.g., IT Risk Policy] | [Organization Name] | [Applicable Sections] |

### 2.4 Reporting Frequency

This report is produced on a **quarterly basis** and tabled at each scheduled Board meeting or Board Risk Committee meeting. Ad-hoc reports may be produced in the event of a material technology risk event or upon request by the Board or regulator.
