## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

*Explain why this document exists and what it is intended to achieve within the organization's risk management framework.*

This Outsourcing Risk Assessment has been prepared by [Organization Name] ("the Institution") in accordance with **Bank Negara Malaysia's (BNM) Risk Management in Technology (RMiT) Policy Document**, specifically **Clause 16.3**, which mandates that licensed financial institutions conduct and document a comprehensive risk assessment for all material outsourcing arrangements.

This document serves the following purposes:

- To systematically identify, assess, and rate risks arising from the outsourcing of [Service/Function] to [Vendor Name];
- To ensure compliance with BNM RMiT Clause 16.3 and all applicable subsidiary requirements;
- To provide the Board of Directors and Senior Management with a structured risk profile of the outsourcing arrangement;
- To establish the basis for ongoing monitoring, control, and governance of the third-party relationship;
- To satisfy BNM notification and reporting obligations where applicable; and
- To document the Institution's due diligence conducted prior to and during the outsourcing engagement.

### Appendix G — Glossary of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| BNM | Bank Negara Malaysia — the central bank and financial regulator of Malaysia |
| RMiT | Risk Management in Technology — BNM policy document governing technology risk management for financial institutions |

| Fourth Party | A sub-contractor or supplier engaged by the primary vendor (third party) to fulfil obligations under the outsourcing arrangement |
| RTO | Recovery Time Objective — the target duration within which a system or service must be restored following a disruption |
| RPO | Recovery Point Objective — the maximum acceptable amount of data loss measured in time in the event of a disruption |
| SLA | Service Level Agreement — the contractual commitment to defined levels of service between the Institution and the vendor |
| KPI | Key Performance Indicator — a measurable value used to evaluate the performance of the vendor |
| RACI | Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed — a framework for defining roles in a process or activity |
| PDPA | Personal Data Protection Act 2010 — Malaysian legislation governing the processing of personal data |
| NACSA | National Cyber Security Agency of Malaysia |
| CISO | Chief Information Security Officer |
| CRO | Chief Risk Officer |
| DPO | Data Protection Officer |
| SOC 2 | Service Organization Control 2 — an auditing standard for assessing the security and availability controls of service organizations |
| ISO 27001 | International standard for information security management systems |
| ISO 22301 | International standard for business continuity management systems |
| Tiering | The process of classifying outsourcing arrangements into risk categories to determine the appropriate level of oversight |
| Due Diligence | A systematic assessment conducted to evaluate the capabilities, risks, and compliance posture of a vendor |
